# Templates

Slider Templates are essentially ready-made sliders from the Azwedo team for the community to use. It requires no understanding of the app and can save you plenty of time.

Some of the templates are free, while others are premium. The templates are listed on the `Templates tab on the home of the slider app. To use them is very simple, You click copy and paste anywhere in Webflow, and you are ready to go.
