# Future

We are working hard to bring you the best experience with our Sliders and we are taking the necessary steps, with dedication, to achieve that mission. In the near future, we plan to address and improve upon key areas we believe will benefit the community:

- New Premium and Free Templates
- Slide Indicators: Clickable slides that change upon click
- Allow default slide animations to occur on click
- More transition effects on Slider Type, such as cubic, zoom, etc.

We welcome additional features requested by the community to meet their needs. Please feel free to contact us, share your ideas, and contribute to the community. Valuable ideas may be rewarded with premium slider templates. So, don't hesitate to get in touch!
